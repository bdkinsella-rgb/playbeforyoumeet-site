Common questions

The games aren't opening. Make sure the add-on has been granted Calendar permissions. Try removing and reinstalling the add-on.

I'm not getting reminder emails. Open the add-on Settings and confirm that "Reminder emails" is toggled on and a timing is selected.

How do I uninstall? Go to your Google Workspace Marketplace apps, find Play Before You Meet, and click Remove.
