Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Play Before You Meet ("the add-on") is a Google Calendar add-on that helps you focus or relax with a quick game before meetings. This policy explains what data the add-on accesses and how it is used.

Data we access

  • Calendar event titles and start times — used to display your upcoming meeting in the sidebar and to time the game countdown.
  • Your Google account email address — used solely to send you reminder emails when you enable that feature.

Data we do not collect

The add-on does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data remains within Google's infrastructure (Apps Script and Gmail). No analytics, tracking, or advertising is used.

Reminder emails

If you enable reminder emails in Settings, the add-on sends an email to your own Google account before each meeting. You can disable this at any time from the add-on's Settings panel.

Third-party sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your data to any third parties.
